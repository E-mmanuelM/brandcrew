System / Pipeline Flow

Weekly Pipeline

Every week, 7 agents and 1 human collaborate to produce polished LinkedIn posts with branded images. Sunday: research, strategy, idea selection. Mon-Fri: daily writing, scoring, approval, and image generation. Humans gate every critical decision.

STEPS: 9
AGENTS: 7
HUMAN GATES: 2
CYCLE: WEEKLY

You review all 10 ideas on Telegram and multi-select your picks (e.g. "1,3,5,7,9"). Pick more from the same batch, reply MORE for a fresh batch, or DONE to lock the week. Approved count always comes from database.

FT.04 Content Creator Daily (Mon–Fri)
Writes the post in your configured voice. Picks the oldest approved idea (FIFO queue). The idea is injected into the prompt to prevent drift. Loads voice profile, quality standards, and Marketing Director's weekly direction. Auto-chains to Editor on completion.

FT.05 Content Editor Auto-chains
Scores the draft on 5 dimensions — voice match, usefulness, specificity, hook quality, and slop filter. Each scored 0-10. Pass threshold is 35/50. On pass, sends the full post text + score to Telegram for human approval. On fail, returns to Writer with specific feedback.
