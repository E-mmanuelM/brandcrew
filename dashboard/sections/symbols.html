Full-Time Agents FT.01 — FT.07 / VPS Cron / Always On

The operational pipeline. These agents run on the server via cron schedule — researching, directing, writing, scoring, designing, and repurposing every week. They communicate through shared files and database, never directly.

FT.01
Niche Content Researcher
Scans niche weekly for trending topics and saves to database with relevance scores
FT.02
Marketing Director
Reads all intelligence and synthesizes into weekly direction for the pipeline
FT.03
Content Strategist
Generates 10 distinct content ideas from research for human multi-select
FT.04
Content Creator
Writes posts in your configured voice from the selected idea
FT.05
Content Editor
Scores drafts on 5 dimensions — pass/fail quality gate at 35/50
FT.06
Social Media Designer
Selects templates and generates post designs via HTML templates + Playwright
FT.07
Content Repurposer
Adapts approved posts into platform-native formats for X, newsletters, blog
Part-Time Agents PT.01 — PT.04 / Manual / Intelligence Layer

Run manually on local machine for deep research. These produce the foundational intelligence that full-time agents depend on — creator maps, content gap analysis, cross-market formats.

PT.01
Niche Creator Researcher
Maps top creators in your configured niche
PT.02
Content Performance Analyst
Analyzes what content performs and why across the niche
PT.03
Cross-Market Researcher
Finds high-performing formats outside the niche to transplant
PT.04
Lead Agent
Coordinates research team and synthesises findings into master reports
Human Touchpoints HU.01 / Decision Gates

You in the loop. The system never acts autonomously on critical decisions — idea selection, draft approval, and LinkedIn posting always require human judgment. Filled circles distinguish humans from agent outlines.

HU.01
You (Operator)
System operator — selects ideas, approves drafts, gives feedback, directs strategy, posts to LinkedIn
