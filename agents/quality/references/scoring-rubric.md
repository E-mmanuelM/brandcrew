# Quality Agent — Detailed Scoring Rubric

> Full rubric for scoring drafts. Use this when the quick SKILL.md criteria aren't enough to make a call.

## Dimension 1: Voice Match (0-10)

**10:** Unmistakably sounds like a practitioner who has done the work. Specific vocabulary, direct sentences, takes a position.
**7-9:** Mostly sounds right, minor generic moments that could be tightened.
**4-6:** Could have been written by anyone in the content owner's field. Not wrong, but not distinctively theirs.
**1-3:** Generic LinkedIn professional voice. Polished but impersonal.
**0:** AI-obvious. Reads like a ChatGPT output with no editing.

## Dimension 2: Genuine Usefulness (0-10)

**10:** Someone in the target audience reads this and immediately thinks of something to apply.
**7-9:** Good information, actionable for most readers.
**4-6:** Interesting but abstract — no clear takeaway.
**1-3:** Surface-level observation. Doesn't tell the reader anything they don't already know.
**0:** No useful information. Pure positioning content.

## Dimension 3: Specificity (0-10)

**10:** Contains specific numbers, tools, company types, scenarios, or processes only an insider would know.
**7-9:** Has some specific details, could use more.
**4-6:** Mostly general statements with occasional specifics.
**1-3:** All generalities. No grounding in real operational reality.
**0:** Pure abstractions. No specifics anywhere.

## Dimension 4: Hook Quality (0-10)

**10:** Forces the reader to stop. Creates immediate curiosity or pattern interrupt.
**7-9:** Good hook, earns the read.
**4-6:** Fine but forgettable. Won't stop the scroll.
**1-3:** Weak opener — question, vague statement, or "I" start.
**0:** Active deterrent. The first line makes you want to keep scrolling.

## Dimension 5: No Slop Markers (0-10)

**10:** Zero banned phrases. Reads like a real person.
**7-9:** Clean with 1-2 slightly generic phrases that aren't on the hard ban list.
**4-6:** Contains soft slop — padded language, unnecessary filler.
**1-3:** Contains 1-2 hard banned phrases.
**0:** Multiple hard banned phrases. Immediately recognizable as AI.

## Failure Feedback Template

When sending back to Writer Agent:
```
FAIL — Score: [X]/50

Specific issues:
- [Dimension]: [Exact problem] — [What to fix]
- [Dimension]: [Exact problem] — [What to fix]

Keep: [What worked — be specific]
Fix: [What needs to change — be specific]

Retry [1 or 2] of 2.
```
