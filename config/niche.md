## Topic Acceptance Categories (Research Agent filter)

The Research Agent uses these categories to decide which trending content to accept or reject.
A topic MUST fall into at least one of these categories to be saved. Update these to match YOUR niche.

1. **Tools and technology in practice** — any SaaS or growth tool being used in a real company. Tutorials, honest reviews, implementation stories, failure reports. Not vendor announcements — real practitioner experience.
2. **Growth and revenue mechanics** — how SaaS companies are actually growing. Pricing changes, onboarding experiments, churn analysis, activation improvements. Must have specific numbers or a real story, not abstract theory.
3. **Founder and operator reality** — real people sharing what actually happened when they tried something. Build-in-public stories, honest post-mortems, "here's what we got wrong" content. Confessions beat case studies.
4. **AI applied to SaaS operations** — how AI tools are changing product development, customer success, sales, support, or internal operations at SaaS companies. Specific use cases, not "AI will change everything."
5. **Metrics and measurement** — posts about specific metrics, dashboards, frameworks for tracking growth. ARR breakdowns, cohort analyses, NRR benchmarks. Data-driven content that practitioners can learn from.
